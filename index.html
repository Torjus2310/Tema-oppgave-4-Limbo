Limbo
The developers at PlayDead Studios have gone on record saying there is no official meaning to Limbo or its ending, making it ripe for personal speculation. The only sanctioned story is “Unsure of his sister’s fate, a boy enters limbo.”

The word Limbo is also known as "the Rift plain" it is the plain of existence between the Material Plain (Life, the universe, everything you feel, see, hear etc.) and the Ethereal plain (ghosts, wraiths etc.). It is often said that Limbo is where your souls go after death while waiting to be sent to Heaven or Hell. A soul needs to pass the state of limbo to move on...

Playdead
Playdead is an independent game developer and publisher based in Copenhagen, Denmark. The company was founded in 2006 by game designer Arnt Jensen. Since then, we have made LIMBO and INSIDE. Today, we are a team of 40 creative professionals, coming from a broad range of experience and education.
